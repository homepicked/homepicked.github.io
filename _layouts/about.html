About HomePicked

Rooms we imagine,
pieces you can own

HomePicked is a blog about the spaces we live in — rendered, curated, and made real. We create AI-rendered interiors and then find every piece that makes them come to life.

No fluff, no filler. Just the actual products behind the look, chosen because they work — not because they pay well.

AI Renders Real Products Honest Picks Home Decor

✦Carefully Rendered

Every room starts as an AI render — a mood, a palette, a vision. We build spaces that feel real before a single product is bought.

✦Genuinely Picked

When we share a product it's because it fits — the right texture, the right scale, the right price. We only recommend what we'd actually use.

✦Timeless Over Trendy

Trends fade. We focus on pieces that age well — warm materials, clean lines, things that still feel right in five years.
