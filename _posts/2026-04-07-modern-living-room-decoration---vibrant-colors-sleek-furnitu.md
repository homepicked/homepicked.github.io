This modern living room is both stylish and functional. The bold red accent wall draws attention and adds a pop of color to the white walls, while the large window lets ample natural light fill the space. The sleek furniture pieces are made of materials such as wood and metal, giving the room an industrial feel. To recreate this look, consider adding a statement piece like the red accent wall and incorporating a mix of textures with your furniture choices. Don't forget to include plenty of storage solutions to keep clutter at bay in this open-concept living space.
